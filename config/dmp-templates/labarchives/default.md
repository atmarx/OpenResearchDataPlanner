## Electronic Lab Notebook (LabArchives)

Research procedures, protocols, and experimental documentation will be
maintained in LabArchives, an electronic lab notebook platform provided
through {{institution.name}}'s institutional license.

**Notebook Allocation:**
- Number of notebooks: {{number service.estimate}}
- Cost: {{currency service.total_cost}} (included with institutional license)

**Appropriate Use:**
LabArchives is used for:
- Experimental protocols and procedures
- Daily lab notes and observations
- Small supporting files and images
- Documentation of methodologies

**Storage Limitations:**
LabArchives has fixed storage constraints:
- Maximum file size: 4 GB (250 MB via auto-uploader)
- Maximum storage per notebook: 1 TB
- Not suitable for large datasets

Large datasets, raw instrument output, and bulk data files are stored
on dedicated research storage infrastructure, with references maintained
in the lab notebook.

**Data Integrity:**
- Complete version history and audit trail
- Timestamped entries for research integrity
- Witness and signature functionality available
- Export to PDF for archival purposes

**Access Control:**
Notebook access is managed through LabArchives' permission system.
The PI controls membership and can set read-only or edit permissions
per user. All access and modifications are logged.

**Long-Term Access:**
Lab notebooks will be retained for the required retention period.
At project conclusion, notebooks can be exported to PDF format and
archived per institutional records retention policies.
