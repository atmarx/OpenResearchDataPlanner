**Engagement Model:**
Consultation hours can be used flexibly throughout the grant period.
Hours are available in blocks of 5 ($750) or 10 ($1,500) for ongoing
engagements. The complimentary first hour applies to each new project.

**Scheduling:**
Consultation appointments are scheduled through the Research Computing
portal. For urgent needs, contact research-computing@northwinds.edu.

**Deliverables:**
Consultation engagements may include written recommendations,
architecture diagrams, sample code, or documentation depending
on the scope of work.
