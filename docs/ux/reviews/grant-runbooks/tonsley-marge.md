**Steps:**

1. Open the tool → **Start New Plan**
2. **Tier:** I click "Check My Tier" because I do not know what tier this should be.  I answer each question carefully.  Not human subjects — these are 800-year-old manuscripts.  No industry partner.  No government restrictions.  No export controls.  No proprietary concerns.  → **Low Risk**.  That makes sense.  Medieval manuscripts are not a security threat.

6. **Software:** I skip this.  Petra uses scanning software but it is on the scanner machine.
7. **Retention:** Federal grant standard.  3 years seems right for an NEH grant.
8. **Results:** I click the DMP tab.  There is text there.  It seems reasonable.  I copy it to a Word document.  I will email it to Petra to check.

**What I want out of this:** A data management section for the NEH application.  Something I can show to the grants office.  Petra will review it.
